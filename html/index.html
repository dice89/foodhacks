What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is a social event where interdisciplinary teams work together for one weekend to build innovative prototypes. Therefore, it is not a classic programming contest, itis more a collective social experience: a festival of IT-culture

Goal of the Event

Food{Hack}'s is to bring together programmers, designers and industry experts to build disruptive and innovative digitial prototypes will be developed that migh include: apps that help farmers, to market their products, analyses of supply chains of large retailers, solutations to alleviate world hunger, or an innovative delivery service app.

Food{Hacks}’s goal is to bring together programmers, designers and industry experts to build disruptive and innovative digital prototypes for the food industry. To solve industry problems, different prototypes will be developed that might include: apps that help farmers to directly market their product, analyses of supply chains of large retailers, solutions to alleviate world hunger, or yet another innovative delivery service app.

For potential partners the main benefit lies within the scouting of young and creative IT students or other digital natives who are able to tackle and solve problems with fresh ideas outside of typical thought patterns.

Food

How much space for innovation lies in food?

How can you start at this hackathon?

Here you will find different ideas for inspiration about how broad the field of the food industry is. There is unlimted amount of pototential to be unlocked during the hackathon

Delivery

Getting fresh food to consumer in the fastest possible way allows us to have the greatest experience in the richness and freshness of what our planet has to offer. Of course this means a huge challenge from the farmer to the delivery service to have the greatest results. Get ready for 7 billion peoples wanted to have a good diary.

Marketing

Did you know there are more than 50 known kinds of carrots in all different sizes and colors?Maybe if you were able to choose between them, you would possibly be able to buy them.

Digitilization

Wouldn‘t it be great if we knew more about every detail we eat? Starting from the soil our vegetables have grown, to the farming conditions all over to what molecules are actually inside. Wouldnt it be great for us to know what we still have to eat today and what ingredients we should not eat anymore?

Social Foodia

Never has humanity been so thankful to get instagram from all friends about their special dinner, which was most of the time some ordinary hamburger or salad. Are we capable of come up with something better than a collective mass-sharing of food pics?

Design

When it comes to designing food, Chefs from all over the world come up with the most creative ideas. What if technology helps us create new designs with existing food ressources? Fun fact: there was a project with 3D-printed cookies made from flour worms. A high protein insect delicacy.

Restaurant Finding

Could we invent a new way of discovering better places to get better food for less money?

Hyperlocalization

Why do we eat apples from the other side of the globe when they grow on trees outside our city?Please do something, this is ridiculous.

Preparation

Ever touched a screen with in dough dripping fingers, because you needed to see your recipe app? Probably not, because you can‘t cook ;) But others have those and similar problems everyday and our technology is not prepared for this – yet. What if we go much further than just thinking about how to communicate processing steps for preparation, but invent systems for automatic principles which are so simple every user can understand?
