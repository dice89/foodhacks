After many sucessful events in Souther Germany HackerStolz e.V. (www.hackerstolz.de) will bring his hackathon series to Berlin and will host Food{Hacks} in 2016.

The event will take place at the Rainmaking Loft which is located in the Charlottenstraße 2, 10969 Berlin

What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is a social event where interdisciplinary teams work together for one weekend to build innovative prototypes. Therefore, it is not a classic programming contest, itis more a collective social experience: a festival of IT-culture

Goal of the Event

Food{Hack}'s is to bring together programmers, designers and industry experts to build disruptive and innovative digitial prototypes will be developed that migh include: apps that help farmers, to market their products, analyses of supply chains of large retailers, solutations to alleviate world hunger, or an innovative delivery service app.

Food{Hacks}’s goal is to bring together programmers, designers and industry experts to build disruptive and innovative digital prototypes for the food industry. To solve industry problems, different prototypes will be developed that might include: apps that help farmers to directly market their product, analyses of supply chains of large retailers, solutions to alleviate world hunger, or yet another innovative delivery service app.

For potential partners the main benefit lies within the scouting of young and creative IT students or other digital natives who are able to tackle and solve problems with fresh ideas outside of typical thought patterns.
